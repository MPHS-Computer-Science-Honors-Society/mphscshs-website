< Attendence Recovery >

Collectively being on the same page is paramount for all members in the club; that way, the club can teach and perform activities effectively and efficiently. For this reason, members are not permitted more than 3 unexcused absenses, and they are allowed no more than 8 absences in total. In order to review any information missed during a meeting, members can check the documents tab listed for each meeting date, allowing them to access any slides or activities that happened that day. If needed, members can then complete the form below under the same date, answering some questions about what they learned from the document to apply for excusal. The secretary will review the form for excusal and will permit it under their own discretion.
