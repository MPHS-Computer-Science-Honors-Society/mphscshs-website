About Coding Club

We are the Myers Park Honor Society Coding Club! Our goal is to provide an opportunity for people to dive into their interest in Computer Science without any experience. We strive to give students a safe place to have fun and create connections while building the subject of Computer Science at Myers Park High School.

This is our first year here at Myers Park, but all of our leaders have been working hard to make sure it is a great year for everyone in the club. You can learn more about the Coding Club in the button below.
