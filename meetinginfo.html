Absences

If you have more than 3 unexcused absences, you will be recieving an email sometime this week about attendance makeup. We will provide you with the dates in which you were absent.

If you have 3 unexcused absences, we will also send a warning email, suggesting you do makeup attendance.

If you have more than 3 unexcused absences by the time of the election, you will not be eligible to run for leadership.

If you have more than 3 unexcused absences or 8+ total absences by the end of the year, you will not recieve credit for CSHS!
